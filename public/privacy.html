Privacy & Data Protection

Effective date: 2025-12-13

This page explains how Knowledge Companion handles your conversations, files, and “memories”. Your actual data flows depend on which providers you configure.

What Data The App Handles

  • Account data (if enabled): email/ID and authentication tokens via an auth provider (e.g., Supabase).
  • Conversation content: messages you type and AI responses you receive.
  • Files and notes: documents you attach/embed and any notes you write in the app.
  • Memory entries: text and metadata saved into your configured knowledge store (e.g., Qdrant).

Where Your Data Goes

When you enable features, your data is sent to the services you configure, such as:

  • AI providers (MiniMax, Grok, Gemini)
  • Vector database (Qdrant / Qdrant Cloud)
  • Embedding provider (Cohere)
  • Web search provider (Tavily)
  • Auth provider (Supabase)

Model Training

  • We do not use your data to train or improve any models.
  • Third‑party providers may have their own retention/training policies. Review their terms and use opt‑out settings where available.

Retention & Deletion

  • On-device: the app may store settings and conversation state on your device.
  • Remote: memories are stored in the remote services you configure.
  • Backups/snapshots: deleted data may remain in backups until they expire per provider retention.

Security

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) where supported by the provider.
  • At‑rest encryption/access controls depend on the provider and your configuration.
